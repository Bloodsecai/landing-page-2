About Me

I’m Mailyn, founder ng ApoyoFilipino at isang DELE A2 passer. I personally took and passed the DELE A2 exam as a requirement for Spanish nationality, and that experience inspired me to create this course.

Ginawa ko ito para tulungan ang mga busy na Filipinos na mag-review nang malinaw at structured with proven strategies, real exam methods, and step-by-step guidance.

Sa ApoyoFilipino, may malinaw kang direksyon at may kasama kang gagabay hanggang sa makamit mo ang goal mo na pumasa.

Dele A2 Review Course

The DELE A2 Review Course by Mailyn of ApoyoFilipino is a self-placed online course designed for busy Filipinos preparing for the DELE A2 exam for Spanish nationality.

It covers all four exam parts: Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking-using clear strategies, real exam methods, and step-by-step guidance so learners can prepare efficiently and pass with confidence

Prueba 1 — Reading

  • DELE A2 Reading Comprehension (Prueba 1) Introduction
  • Test Structure and Timing
  • Key Strategies for Success
  • Time Management Tips
  • Scoring and Content Overview
  • Answer Sheet Guidelines
  • Test Day Instructions
  • Task 1 – Email-Based Questions
  • Task 2 – Short Texts / Advertisements
  • Task 3 – Matching Statements
  • Task 4 – Long Text / Journalistic Article
  • Common Topics in Reading Texts
  • Extra Tips – Handling Distractors and Synonyms
  • Preparing Mentally for Listening Test

Prueba 2 — Listening

  • DELE A2 Listening (Prueba 2): 4 tasks
  • Preparation: manage time, check audio, memorize instructions, highlight keywords
  • Tasks:
  • T1: conversations + images
  • T2: news / announcements
  • T3: statement matching & synonyms
  • T4: short messages & main ideas
  • Strategies: use keywords/initials, focus on who/when/where/how
  • Listening: first = general idea, second = details
  • Skills: eliminate distractors, recognize tone (news, advice, ads)
  • Practice: mini simulations, active listening, use breaks wisely

Prueba 3 — Writing

  • DELE A2 Writing (Prueba 3):
  • overview of tasks and time management
  • Exam basics: use scratch paper, write clearly on official sheets
  • Tasks:
  • Tarea 1: reply to a message (email, note, postcard)
  • Tarea 2: choose one option (personal life or another person via photos)
  • Writing tips: clear handwriting, correct spelling, accents, punctuation
  • Strategies: plan, organize, respect word count, use connectors
  • Grammar focus: pretérito imperfecto
  • Content: greetings, likes, proposals, questions, closings
  • Avoid: common mistakes
  • Finish: quick checklist before submitting

Prueba 4 — Speaking

  • DELE A2 Speaking (Prueba 4):
  • exam context, structure, and logistics
  • Tasks:
  • Tarea 1: short oral presentation
  • Tarea 2: photo description
  • Tarea 3: dialogue with examiner
  • Preparation: use templates, plan in preparation room
  • Exam tips: Tú vs. Usted, stay relaxed and confident
  • Strategies: connectors, clear structure, simple verb tenses
  • Interaction: warm-up questions, positive attitude
  • Support: filler phrases, ask for clarification, recover from mistakes
